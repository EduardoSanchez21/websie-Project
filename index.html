The history and start of Steroids

Steroids are known around the world as a drug used to boost your testosterone levels, leading to higher and better physical performance. It is believed that the first people to deal with this drug was the soviet union and other countries such as, eastern germany. At the time of its creation it was being used as a way to get better performance out of olympic weightlifters of the 1940's. Since then a very wide variety of steroids have been Made alongside the now large amount of people who use it, now not only for a boost in muscle power but also as a way to treat certain sicknesses and diseases. But the question is if it is actually doing more bad than good.
